Notes to Grader:
- [Freeze!] button stops all animations.
- [Keep breathing] button lets the dog breathe.
- [Start walking] button makes the dog walk.
- There are buttons to turn animation on and off on each leg.
- Front legs have third level joints (feet).
- Pyramids are used for the ears and the tail.
- Camera angle can be rotated by clicking and dragging the mouse on the canvas.
- If you shift+click the dog, the dog will wag his tail.


Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
